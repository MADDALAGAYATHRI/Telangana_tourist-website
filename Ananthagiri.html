About

Ananthagiri Hills, located in Telangana, is a peaceful getaway surrounded by nature. It's a great place for people who love trees, fresh air, and beautiful views. You can walk through the trees, sleep in a tent outside, or just enjoy the quietness. There's also an old temple on top of the hills that you can visit. Ananthagiri Hills is perfect for anyone who wants to relax and enjoy nature's beauty.Visitors to Ananthagiri Hills can explore the lush green forests and take leisurely walks along the winding paths. The cool breeze and serene atmosphere make it an ideal destination for a refreshing retreat amidst nature.
